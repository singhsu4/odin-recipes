My Favorite Cocktail, A French 75

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces of lemon juice
  • 2 fluid ounces of gin
  • 2 teaspoons of simple syrup
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 4 fluid ounces of champagne
  • 1 lemon or orange slice for garnish
  • 1 maraschino cherry for garnish

Instructions

Mix lemon juice, gin, simply syrup, and ice cubes together in a shaker. Shake for several seconds. Then pour into a glass. Add Garnishes and enjoy!
