Da Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  1. Spicy cheese
  2. Cream Cheese
  3. Special bread from your favorite local bakery
  4. butter

Directions

Slice the bread. Add a generous amount of spicy cheese to your build (at least 2 slices). Spread some cream cheese on the top of this. Add the other piece of bread. Butter the outer sides. Place on a non-stick pan, grill each side until golden and the cheese is melted. Then Enjoy!
